    Chapters

    With its institute chapter program, Blockchain At India is providing unique opportunities to students for networking, mentoring and bonding over common interests. Chapters provide support to student community as well as to non institutional local communities. With over 5 active chapters, B@I is on a mission to support institutions all over india to develop the ecosystem for blockchain development

    Our Partners

    Our parent organisation ZUBI brings some of the leading blockchain companies, projects, internships and seminars to student communities. Our partners are pledged to provide necessary resources to create receptiveness, standards, education and technology for the growth of Blockchain in India.

    Contribute to Community

    With growing of this community, we would like to have more no of enthusiast community members to provide resources for the mission of creating India's largest student driven blockchain community. Providing contributions has its own benifits, which will be mailed after first contribution.

    Our Mission

    We at Zubi.io are at a mission to create India's largest student-driven community for emerging technologies. We are starting with blockchain as blockchain is the recent blooming technology with very promising future and high developers demand in the market. We launched this platform a few months back and have already registered more than 400 professionals from institutes like Harvard Business School, Imperial College London, IIT's, Google, Microsoft and many more from around the globe. B@I is our Child branch, which is responsible for managing students chapter across pan India. Click at the link to be a part of ZUBI as an expert, mentor, developer or a learner.
